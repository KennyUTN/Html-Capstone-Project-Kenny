About us

Swing Thing 2020 is an annual three-day music festival in Chascomus, Buenos Aires at Sisi Park, founded in 2016 and since expanded to other countries. Performances include but are not limited to jazz, electro swing, dubstep and tecno. The three-day music festival in Chascomus hosts an estimated 400,000 people each year and sells out annually. Swing Thing is considered one of the largest and most iconic music festivals in the world and one of the longest running in Argentina.
